FAQ

Quick answers to the most common questions.

How do I fund my wallet?
Go to Wallet, enter an amount, and use Paystack. After payment, verify and your balance updates.
Do you support multiple networks?
Yes. AxisVTU supports MTN and Glo in the current flow, and can be expanded to more networks.
Where can I find receipts?
Open Transactions and click a transaction to view the receipt modal. You can copy references for reconciliation.
What happens if my session expires?
The app handles token refresh and will prompt you to log in again if your session can’t be recovered.
Can I set reseller margins?
Yes. Admin users can manage pricing rules by role and network.
Is the pricing final?
This page is a starting template. Update pricing and terms based on your provider costs and business plan.
